❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Is StreamWheel free to use?
Yes! The core app — Classic & Elimination modes are free to use, although in the future we might bring in premium functions to cover the costs. Check the Premium page for advanced features.
My data disappeared — what happened?
A lot of basic data is stored in your local browser, this means changing from device or browser will not carry over this data, we do this to keep the costs for running a database as log as possible. In the future we plan to bring more data into a database to allow streamers to use streamwheel from any place at any time.
Can I suggest a feature?
Absolutely! Post it in our Discord server under #feature-requests, or email here. I read everything.
When is the Xsee API integration coming?
For now we are waiting on the Xsee team to have the developed API available and intagrate it in our platform. Update Log.
I found a bug — how do I report it?
Email here with "BUG:" in the subject. Please include your browser and what you were doing when it happened.
