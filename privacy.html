Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: April 12, 2026 🎡 StreamWheel
👋 Introduction

Welcome to StreamWheel. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

StreamWheel is a streamer giveaway tool. By using our service, you agree to the practices described in this document.

💬 Questions? Reach us at gmail
1 Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account data — name, email address, and social login identifiers (Twitter/X, Discord, Xsee) when you register.
  • Usage data — game logs, winner history, and leaderboard data you create within the app. This is stored locally in your browser by default. In the future this might be liked to a database
  • Technical data — currently none when you visit our website. We use this to improve the service.
  • Communications — any messages you send us via email

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as payment card details directly — payments are handled by a third-party processor.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the StreamWheel service
  • Manage your account and authenticate your identity
  • Sync your game data and leaderboard to the cloud (Premium users)
  • Send service-related communications (account alerts, updates)
  • Improve the app based on usage patterns
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3 Cookies & Local Storage

StreamWheel uses browser localStorage to save your preferences, game logs, leaderboard data, and settings. This data never leaves your device unless you have cloud sync enabled (Premium).

We may use cookies for:

  • Session cookies — to keep you logged in
  • Analytics cookies — anonymous usage statistics (e.g. page views)

You can clear localStorage and cookies at any time through your browser settings. This will reset your local StreamWheel data.

4 Third-Party Services

StreamWheel integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • GitHub — we fetch update and premium content from GitHub (public raw files). No personal data is shared.
  • Twitter/X, Discord, Twitch — optional OAuth login. We only receive your public profile identifier.
  • Xsee — when the API integration is active, participant lists from your live Space are fetched. No data is stored beyond your session.
ℹ️ We have no control over third-party privacy practices. Please review their policies directly.
5 Data Retention & Deletion

Local data (game logs, settings) is stored in your browser indefinitely until you clear it via the app's "Danger Zone" or your browser settings.

If you have a cloud account, you can request full deletion of your account data by emailing us at your@email.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

6 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at your@email.com.

7 Children's Privacy

StreamWheel is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via the in-app Update Log or by email.

The date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last revised. Continued use of StreamWheel after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
